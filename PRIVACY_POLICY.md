# Privacy Policy for Aging Report Application

**Effective Date:** November 7, 2025  
**Last Updated:** November 7, 2025

## Overview
The Aging Report application ("the App") is an internal business tool developed by Alliance Legal Systems Corp. for managing QuickBooks Online aging reports. This privacy policy explains how we handle data accessed through QuickBooks Online.

## Information We Collect
The App accesses the following data from your QuickBooks Online account:
- Customer information (names, balances)
- Invoice data
- Payment records
- Credit memo information
- Journal entries
- Bill payment details

## How We Use Your Information
We use the information solely to:
- Generate aging reports for accounts receivable management
- Analyze payment patterns
- Track outstanding balances
- Provide financial reporting

## Data Storage
- All QuickBooks data is stored securely in Microsoft Azure SQL Database
- OAuth tokens are encrypted and stored securely
- Data is accessed only by authorized personnel within Alliance Legal Systems Corp.

## Data Sharing
We do NOT:
- Sell your data to third parties
- Share your data with external organizations
- Use your data for marketing purposes

## Data Security
- All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
- Database connections are encrypted (TLS/SSL)
- Access is restricted to authorized users only
- OAuth 2.0 is used for secure authentication

## Data Retention
- QuickBooks data is synchronized periodically and stored for reporting purposes
- OAuth tokens are refreshed automatically and expired tokens are removed
- Historical reports may be retained for business records

## Your Rights
You have the right to:
- Revoke app access at any time through your QuickBooks Online account
- Request deletion of your data
- Contact us with questions about data handling

## Third-Party Services
The App uses:
- **QuickBooks Online** - Financial data source (governed by Intuit's privacy policy)
- **Microsoft Azure** - Secure cloud hosting and database services

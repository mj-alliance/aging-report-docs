# Privacy Policy for Aging Report Application

**Effective Date:** November 7, 2025  
**Last Updated:** November 7, 2025

## Overview
The Aging Report application ("the App") is an internal business tool developed by Alliance Legal Solutions, LLC for managing QuickBooks Online aging reports. This privacy policy explains how we handle data accessed from our QuickBooks Online account and what information is stored and processed by the application.

## Information We Access
The App accesses the following data from Alliance Legal Solutions, LLC's QuickBooks Online account:
- Customer information (names, balances)
- Invoice data
- Payment records
- Credit memo information
- Journal entries
- Bill payment details

## How We Use This Information
The information is used solely for:
- Generating aging reports for accounts receivable management
- Analyzing payment patterns
- Tracking outstanding balances
- Providing financial reporting for internal business operations

## Data Storage
- QuickBooks data is stored securely in Microsoft Azure SQL Database
- OAuth tokens are encrypted and stored securely
- Data is accessed only by authorized personnel within Alliance Legal Solutions, LLC
- All data remains under the control of Alliance Legal Solutions, LLC

## Data Sharing
Alliance Legal Solutions, LLC does NOT:
- Sell data to third parties
- Share data with external organizations
- Use data for marketing purposes
- Provide data access to unauthorized parties

## Data Security
- All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
- Database connections are encrypted (TLS/SSL)
- Access is restricted to authorized users only
- OAuth 2.0 is used for secure authentication with QuickBooks Online

## Data Retention
- QuickBooks data is synchronized periodically and stored for reporting purposes
- OAuth tokens are refreshed automatically and expired tokens are removed
- Historical reports may be retained for business records in accordance with company policy

## Internal Use Only
This application is for internal use by Alliance Legal Solutions, LLC employees only. The app:
- Accesses only Alliance Legal Solutions, LLC's own QuickBooks Online data
- Is not made available to external users or customers
- Operates under Alliance Legal Solutions, LLC's internal IT policies

## Third-Party Services
The App relies on:
- **QuickBooks Online (Intuit)** - Financial data source (governed by Intuit's privacy policy and terms of service)
- **Microsoft Azure** - Secure cloud hosting and database services (governed by Microsoft's privacy policy)

## Employee Access Rights
Authorized employees have the right to:
- Use the App for legitimate business purposes
- View reports generated by the App
- Request assistance from IT support for technical issues

Management retains the right to:
- Monitor App usage for security and compliance purposes
- Revoke access for employees who no longer require it
- Audit data access logs
